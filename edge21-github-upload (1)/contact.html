Common Questions

Do I need to be a certain size to work with you?

No. Most of my clients are solopreneurs or small teams of 1–3. If you're making money but drowning in the process, you're the right fit.

What if I just need a template, not full consulting?

That's exactly what The Bold Face Co is for. Browse the Systems page — from mapping kits and SOP templates to the full Freelance Operating System.
