About

Hey there, I'm Christina.

Operational architect. Marketing strategist. The person who sees the gaps you can't — because they live inside your own head.

I don't create templates.
I create operational freedom.

The Bold Face Co is for solopreneurs, creator-adjacent business owners, small local service businesses, and solo-service owners who are excellent at their craft — but tired of running their business from their text messages, memory, and voicemails.

If you're tired of the 14-hour days, surviving on caffeine and screenshots, you've come to the right place.

I've spent years in project management, people management, digital marketing, branding, and social media strategy. But here's what I kept noticing: the freelancers I worked with weren't failing because they lacked talent or clients. They were stalling because they had zero business infrastructure.

Every client felt custom. Every workflow lived in their head. They couldn't delegate, couldn't scale, and couldn't take a day off without everything grinding to a halt.

So I built two things to fix it:

Edge21 Marketing

The Strategy

This is where I work with you directly. I diagnose the operational gaps, design your marketing strategy, position your brand, and build a plan that gets the right clients to your door — and keeps them coming back.

Think of it as the architect's blueprint. We design the foundation before anything gets built.

The Bold Face Co

The Systems

These are the tools to execute. Notion templates, workflow kits, spreadsheets, Canva templates, SOPs, and web apps — built specifically for creator-adjacent freelancers and small business owners who need systems but don't have time to build them from scratch.

Think of it as the contractor's toolkit. Everything you need to build out the blueprint.
